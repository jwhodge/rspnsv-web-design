## Do I really need a website?

Every day having a website becomes more and more critical. It is an essential tool to help you connect with your customers.

Over 80% of people research businesses online before they buy. Over 50% don't trust a company without one.

- Your website makes it easier to:
- Show your products and services
- Show your company's values
- Answer commonly asked questions
- Find your location
- Contact you
